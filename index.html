CV (updated Sep. 2, 2020)


I am currently working as a postdoctoral research fellow in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE) at The University of British Columbia (UBC), advised by Prof. Vincent W.S. Wong.

I received the PhD degree in the Department of Information Engineering (IE) at The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK) in 2018, advised by Prof. Jianwei Huang.

I received the B.S. degree in the Department of Electronics and Information Engineering from Sichuan University (http://eie.scu.edu.cn/eneieen/), China, in 2014.

Research

  • Distributed learning and crowd intelligence

  • Mobile edge computing

  • Mobile video streaming and live streaming

  • Network games and economics
